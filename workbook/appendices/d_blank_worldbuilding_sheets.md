## How To Use These Sheets

- **Copy into your own project file.** One sheet per world element.
- **Fill in what you know; leave blanks where you don't.** Blanks are to-do items, not failures.
- **Return between drafts.** Sheets evolve as the book does.
- **Don't try to fill them all at once.** Do one a day while drafting.
- **Not all sheets apply to all books.** Pick the ones your subgenre demands.
