## Danger Signs Checklist: When You're Info-Dumping

Watch for these in your prose:

☐ Paragraphs with no dialogue or action — just description and explanation
☐ Characters explaining things they both already know — "As you know..."
☐ Thoughts that sound like encyclopedia entries — analytical rather than emotional
☐ Stopping the plot to establish setting — scene goes on pause while you describe
☐ More than three sentences of pure explanation — without grounding it in character
☐ Explaining before showing — telling readers what to expect rather than letting them discover
